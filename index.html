SYNOPSIS

As the skies turn red and the planet trembles, Japan stands on the brink of disaster. However, a determined teenager named Suzume sets out on a mission to save her country. Able to see supernatural forces that others cannot, it's up to her to close the mysterious doors that are spreading chaos across the land. A perilous journey awaits as the fate of Japan rests on her shoulders.

Characters

Munakata Souta
宗像 草太more_vert

View More Info

Munakata Soutacancel

A young man who travels across Japan searching for open doors that bring disaster upon its surroundings. He encounters Suzume while headed for a certain ruin containing a door.

Later Suzume encounters Souta while he is attempting to close a door. With the two's combined efforts they close it. Afterwards Suzume insists on bandaging Souta up at her house. Souta explains that he is a Closer, who's job is to lock doors to the great beyond. This is because a thing called the worm is constantly trying to escape and destroy the earth. He explains that the reason why the worm got loose must have been because the keystone left his post.

Iwato Suzumecancel

Suzume Iwato is a 17-year-old high school girl, who lives with her aunt in a quiet town in the Kyushu region of Japan. While heading to school, she encounters a young man searching for an abandoned area with a door, so she informs him of an abandoned onsen (spa) resort nearby, and curiously follows him.

Beckoned by the mysterious doors, Suzume's "journey of closing doors" begins.

Daijincancel

Daijin is a cat that can speak human language. It is also nicknamed so by locals for its appearance similar to an ancient minister. Daijin is one of the two Keystones in the movie. It has unknowingly been released from its seal by Suzume on its post at the door located at Kyushu.

Iwato Tamakicancel

Tamaki Iwato was born in July 1983, six years after her sister Tsubame Iwato. After Tsubame died in the Great East Japan Earthquake in 2011, Tamaki took in her orphaned niece Suzume and raised her as her daughter in Tonami. She worked in a fishery cooperative along with Minoru Okabe at Tonami Fishing Port.

Serizawa Tomoyacancel

Tomoya came to Souta's apartment looking for him, but he instead met a strange girl named Suzume Iwato, who claimed to be Souta's cousin. They had a brief conversation where Tomoya claimed that Souta owed him 20,000 Yen, however Suzume suddenly ran away before they could talk more.

The next day, he saw her by Ochanomizu Station and offered her a ride to find Souta. They were joined by Suzume's aunt Tamaki Iwato, who initially mistook him for a playboy seducing Suzume, and two cats Daijin and Sadaijin.

About

Suzume no Tojimari (Japanese Name/Suzume's Locking Up) is a 2022 Japanese animated coming-of-age fantasy adventure film written and directed by Makoto Shinkai, produced by CoMix Wave Films, and distributed by Toho.

The film was released in Japan on November 11, 2022, and has received positive reviews from critics and audiences. It has also become one of the highest-grossing Japanese films of all time.
